## Key Takeaways

- Only 1 in 10 people die wealthy because they don't understand the language of money and deals
- You can buy businesses with $0 down using seller financing - 60% of small businesses under $10M use this structure
- Most people have toxic emotional relationships with money that prevent wealth building
- Start by buying businesses in industries you already spend money in to leverage your unfair advantage
- Content creation is the new networking - it's more efficient than traditional sales and opens doors to billionaires
- The biggest wealth secret is turning expenses into assets instead of just paying for services
- B-players in your organization decrease everyone's productivity by 30% - hire A-players or help people improve
- Financial freedom comes from earning, not saving - you cannot save your way to wealth
- Sales is finding people who already want what you're selling, not convincing anyone to change their mind
- Most successful people think 3-5 years ahead while average people focus on short-term tactics

## TLDR

Entrepreneur Codie Sanchez reveals how to build wealth by buying small businesses with little to no money down, using seller financing structures that 60% of businesses offer. She argues most people stay poor because they don't understand deal-making and have toxic emotional relationships with money.
