## Key Takeaways

- Most small business deals can be structured with seller financing, requiring little to no upfront cash
- Look for businesses in areas you already spend money on - turn expenses into assets
- Sales is about finding people already predisposed to buy, not convincing reluctant prospects
- B-players decrease team productivity by 30% and are more dangerous than C-players who get fired quickly
- Content creation is networking at scale - it's more efficient than traditional outbound sales
- The richest 1% will control over half the world's wealth by next year - only 1 in 10 people dies wealthy
- Most people have emotional blocks around money that prevent wealth building
- Exit tax to freedom - there's always a cost to leave comfortable situations for growth
- Billionaires think long-term and look for compound growth over decades, not quick wins

## TLDR

Entrepreneur Codie Sanchez reveals how to buy small businesses with little money using seller financing and deal structuring. She argues most people stay poor due to emotional money blocks and choosing employee mindset over ownership, while demonstrating how content creation can replace traditional networking and sales.
