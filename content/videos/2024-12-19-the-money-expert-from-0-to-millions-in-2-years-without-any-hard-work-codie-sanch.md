## Key Takeaways

- Only 1 in 10 people dies wealthy - most people have unhealthy emotional relationships with money that prevent wealth building
- Small businesses are everywhere and many owners want to sell - you can buy businesses with little to no money down using seller financing
- Start with businesses in industries you already spend money on to leverage your unfair advantage and existing knowledge
- The biggest difference between rich and poor people is understanding 'the language of money' - deal-making and finance aren't complex, they're just made to sound complex
- Content creation is the modern networking - it's more efficient than traditional sales and allows you to attract the right people to you
- Most people stay poor because they follow paths they've seen before and have negative stories about money ingrained from childhood
- True wealth comes from ownership, not employment - shift from being a renter/employee to being an owner of assets that generate income

## TLDR

Entrepreneur Codie Sanchez explains how most people stay poor due to emotional money blocks and lack of deal-making knowledge. She advocates buying small boring businesses with seller financing instead of traditional investing, using your existing industry knowledge as an unfair advantage to build wealth through ownership rather than employment.
